Analysis:

Overall, district schools have lower scores in both reading and math than Charter schools. 
Math and reading score grades stayed pretty consistent from ninth grade to twelfth grade.
Overall, math and reading score grades were higher in schools that spent less money per student. They also had higher passing percentages.
Overall, smaller size schools had higher math and reading score grades. They also had higher passing percentages.
Chater schools had higher average scores in both math and reading, higher passing percentages in math, reading and overall passing percentages than district schools.
